Our Services

We host a variety of services including:

  • Gitea: Self-hosted Git service for developers.
  • Vikunja: Open-source task management tool.
  • Nextcloud: Private cloud storage solution.
  • FreeIPA: Identity management system.
  • HAProxy: High availability load balancer.
  • pfSense: Open-source firewall for network security.
  • Minecraft: Dedicated Minecraft game server.

Each of these services is hosted on our robust infrastructure, ensuring high uptime and fast access for all users.
